How to Donate

Recycle Bicycles gladly accepts donations of bicycles and related equipment, as well as monetary donations to cover expenses. 100% of donations to Recycle Bicycles go to operations. Management and administrative services are donated by volunteers. Our IRS tax ID number (for 501(c)(3) deductions) is 84.1268840. Bikes and related equipment may be dropped off at any of the following locations. THANK YOU to all of these generous partners!

Additional Information

Is my donation tax deductable?

YES! Donations to charitable organizations under federal tax code 501(c)(3) are tax deductible. Our federal tax ID number is 84.1268840

What types of donations do you accept?

We are currently accepting

  • Bicycles, parts, tools, supplies and related equipment
  • Monetary donations
  • Volunteers
  • Bicycle accessories
  • Bicycle locks
  • Bicycle helmets
  • Not on the list? Please contact us and ask!

I know someone who could use a bicycle...do you accept requests?

Yes, please contact us!
